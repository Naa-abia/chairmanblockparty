About

Official Annual event on August 30th to celebrate the life and accomplishments of Chairman Fred Hampton Sr.This event typically takes place in his hometown in the US, but this year we wanted to bring everyone back to our true home to celebrate in Africa.The vision is to do more events like this on the continent of Africa!

Why Africa? Why Ghana?

Chairman Fred Hampton Sr. recently visited Ghana for the first time in December 2021. It was a life changing experience for him and he wants to continue cultivating partnerships on the African continent to expand the Black Panther Cubs’ impact and encourage other people of the Diaspora to connect back home.
